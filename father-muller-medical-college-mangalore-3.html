    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore

    • Sept. 28, 2021, 2:52 a.m.
    • Future education

    Admission 2021-22, Courses, Fees, and much more

    Father Muller Medical College is a Christian minority medical institution established in 1991. A medical college is an integral unit of Father Muller Charitable Institutions, a registered society owned by the Catholic Diocese of Mangalore. 

    Father Muller Medical College popularly abbreviated as FMMC, Mangalore, was established primarily for the benefit of the members of the Christian Community. However, like all other educational institutions under Catholic Community, FMMC is open to all students irrespective of religion, caste, or community as per the guidelines of KEA and NMC altogether.

    As far as Academic programs are concerned, Father Muller Medical College has started Postgraduate degrees in 1991, and later in 1999, MBBS at the Undergraduate level was started with the permission of the National Medical Commission (Earlier MCI), New Delhi. FMMC, Mangalore was recognized by NMC and well approved by MoHFW, Govt. of India.

    Father Muller Medical College is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bengaluru. Several Clinical and Para-Clinical branches of modern medical sciences are offered through MD/MS programs. Besides it, several super specialty programs are also being offered through DM/M.Ch courses.

    FMMC, Mangalore is spread over a green lush area of 30 acres equipped with advanced amenities for the students as well as faculty & staff. As far as accreditation and recognition are concerned, FMMC, Mangalore is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an ‘A’ grade. Independent Private Sector Initiative specializes in rating colleges, and schools have awarded Diamond College Rating to Father Muller Medical College. More details are being shared in the following article.


    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore: Eligibility Criteria

    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore is one of the Top Medical Institutes in Karnataka precisely located in the city of Mangalore. Every medical aspirant has always been looking forward to studying in one of the most reputed Medical Colleges in the country. 

    In order to secure a seat in UG and PG Courses in FMMC, Mangalore, students are required to hold certain eligibility so that they can fulfill their dream of becoming a doctor from one of the most reputed Medical institutes in the Country. FMMC, Mangalore has also been imparting Superspecialty programs through esteemed DM/M.Ch programs. Details of eligibility criteria are being discussed below in the article.

    For MBBS Aspirants:

    • Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English having 50% marks from any recognized board. Candidates from the reserved category need to possess 40% in the same. 
    • Candidates need to attain 17 years of age. Less than mentioned age will not be entertained in any circumstances.
    • Candidates must have qualified NEET-UG with the required percentile. 


    For MD/MS Aspirants:

    • Candidates must have passed MBBS from a recognized University by the NMC.
    • Candidates need to have completed 1 Year of mandatory Internship on or before 31st of March in the same year of applying.
    • Candidates need to produce permanent/provisional registration provided by the NMC (Earlier MCI) or the respective state medical council.
    • Candidates need to obtain a minimum of 50 percentile in NEET-PG for General Category and for reserved category, the requirement is 40 percentile in the same.


    For DM/M.Ch Aspirants:

    • Candidates need to possess a PG Degree in the respective specialization from a recognized medical college by NMC.
    • Candidates need to qualify for NEET-SS conducted by the NBE, New Delhi.
    • For DM Aspirants, MD Degree is essential to possess in the respective branch.
    • For M.Ch Aspirants, MS Degree in the required branch is mandatory to obtain.


    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore: Admission Process

    All the admissions to any of the UG and PG Courses are strictly based on merit gained in the All India Entrance Exam i.e. NEET-UG and NEET-PG for Postgraduate courses. FMMC, Mangalore offers MD and MS Courses in several clinical and para-clinical specializations, recognized by NMC, New Delhi. Apart from these courses, FMMC, Mangalore is also imparting Superspecialty programs through DM/M.Ch programs. 

    Students who wish to take admission in this college, need to obtain the required percentile in NEET followed by the counseling conducted by Karnataka Examination Authority, Bangalore. For All India Quota seats, students need to participate in the counseling conducted by the same. Candidates can certainly visit the official website of the state medical counseling at https://kea.kar.nic.in for more details. Only NEET-qualified candidates are eligible to participate in the counseling. 


    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore: Seat Matrix for Distinct Quotas

    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore is one of the Top and Reputed medical institutes in the state of Karnataka. The college has gained so much appreciation for producing expert medical professionals and humane doctors of distinct specializations. 

    As per the guidelines of the previous MCI (Now NMC), Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore Administration has reserved 85% of All India Quota for State Domicile Category students. According to the NMC guidelines, 15% of seats are reserved for NRI/Foreign Category. 

    As far as the Postgraduate seat matrix in FMMC, Mangalore is concerned, 40% of total intake is reserved under Govt. quota, and for the Private/General quota, 35% of total seats are reserved. There is a separate Management/NRI quota under which, candidates across the globe can claim an MD/MS seat in the 25% NRI quota. Necessary details along with course streams are mentioned below.


    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore: MBBS Admission Process

    Being one of the Premier Medical Colleges in Karnataka, Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore offers Bachelor’s Degree in Medicine and Surgery. The college offers an MBBS Program that carries a duration of 4.5 years of Academic Course and 1 year of Mandatory Internship. The entire duration of the course is 5.5 years. 

    Aspirants seeking admission to FMMC, Mangalore need to qualify for NEET-UG. Candidates are required to go through a centralized Counselling process conducted by KEA, Bangalore. 

    All rounds of Counselling are held online. However, if seats remain vacant after the Final/Mop-Up Round of Counselling conducted by KEA, Bangalore then Candidates are called as per their NEET Rank for the stray round of Counselling conducted by the college itself as per the guidelines laid down by the NMC, New Delhi. The entire Counselling process has been discussed in the article. Candidates then need to report to the college with having certain essential documents.

    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore: MD/MS Admission Process

    Being one of the Top Medical Colleges in Karnataka, Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore is widely acclaimed for its postgraduate courses in Medical Science. FMMC, Mangalore offers MD Degree in 13 distinct branches. It offers General Medicine, Pathology, Dermatology, Paediatrics, Orthopaedics, and more specialties with a duration of 3 years. 

    KIMS, Bangalore also offers MS courses in 5 Specialities. The duration of the course is 3 years. Students across the country seriously look forward to earning MD and MS degrees from this college. 

    Aspirants are supposed to have completed 1 year of mandatory Internship and must possess Provisional/Permanent Medical Registration from the respective medical council of the state before taking admission to any of the Postgraduate Courses in this reputed Medical Institute. 


    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore: DM/M.Ch Admission Process

    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore is one of the very first colleges in the state of Karnataka to have granted permission to carry out Superspecialty programs in several specializations. DM Program is conducted in FMMC, Mangalore in Neurology and Nephrology. 

    To pursue the mentioned DM Program, one needs to qualify NEET-SS conducted by NBE with the required percentile and aspirants to need to acquire MD Degree in the required branch. 

    FMMC, Mangalore is also known for its advanced M.Ch program in Urology. For pursuing M.Ch from the reputed FMMC, one needs to have MS Degree in the required branch.

    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore: Superspecialty Courses

    Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore is an integral unit of Father Muller Charitable Institution. The University is widely regarded for its super specialty programs in almost every clinical and non-clinical branch of medical sciences. DM program along with M.Ch courses are conducted in Father Muller Medical College, Mangalore.

    Most of these courses are attained by the DNB Candidates and MD/MS qualified candidates. The courses are highly demanding and highly acclaimed for their scope in future aspects for teaching and expertise professionals in distinct specializations. 

    DM programs can be pursued by an MD Degree holder in the respective branch of medicine. However, for the M.Ch program, MS is required to pursue the course with the required branch’s demand. 

    All the admissions to the super specialty courses are strictly based on NEET-SS conducted by NBE (National Board of Examinations) and followed by the counseling conducted by DGHS, on behalf of MCC, New Delhi.
